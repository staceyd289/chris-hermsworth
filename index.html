About Chris Hemsworth

Chris Hemsworth is an award-winning Australian actor, widely recognized for his role as Thor in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Beyond acting, he is a producer, fitness entrepreneur, and global ambassador for wellness and performance. Through his platform and fitness brand Centr, he inspires millions to live healthier, more balanced lives.
